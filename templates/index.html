Steam Crawler

Welcome to Steam Crawler

How to Use:

  • Input Your Preferences:
    Begin by entering your preferences, such as game genre, tags, or keywords, into the search bar.
  • Build Your Steam Store URL:
    Our smart algorithm will use your input to construct a tailored Steam Store URL.
  • Retrieve Up to 100 Search Results:
    Click on the "Search" button to fetch up to 100 search results from the Steam Store based on your preferences.
  • Explore the Results:
    Browse through the list of games to discover titles that match your interests.
  • Access Detailed Game Information:
    Click on a game title to access its Steam Store page and unveil more details.
  • Export Everything:
    Export everything, such as reviews, ratings, descriptions, into CSV format
  • Refine Your Search:
    If you want to adjust your preferences, simply go back, modify your input, and click "Search" again.
Note:
  • Some games may have restricted access, and certain details may not be available for extraction.
  • Make sure to respect the terms of service of the Steam platform while using this tool.

About Steam Crawler

Welcome to Steam Crawler, a project designed to explore the world of web scraping and enhance the gaming discovery experience on the Steam platform.

Our Mission: This project aims to study web scraping techniques by providing users with a tool to discover Steam games based on their preferences.

Why the Limits: To ensure a responsible and respectful use of resources, Steam Crawler imposes some limits on the number of requests made to the Steam servers. These limits help prevent excessive load on the servers and ensure a smooth experience for all users.

Please Note:
  • Be mindful of the number of requests you make to avoid overloading the Steam servers.
  • This project is for educational and exploratory purposes and is not intended for commercial use.
  • Respect the terms of service of the Steam platform while using this tool.
